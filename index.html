Unicurn

The UCR-1 basket contains:

  • 0.1856 USD
  • 0.1607 EUR
  • 0.7831 CNY
  • 5.441 JPY
  • 0.0216 GBP
  • 1.534 INR
  • 0.0565 BRL
  • 0.0205 CAD
  • 16.51 KRW
  • 0.7583 RUB
  • 0.0167 AUD
  • 0.2334 MXN

This means its value is roughly 0.65 USD, or 0.61 EUR, etc. It can be used as a unit of value when choosing a particular nation state's currency is undesirable. In that sense, transactions in UCR-1 are like conversations in Esperanto.

UCR-1 was defined on [date to be determined] and currencies represented in the basket are roughly rated by nominal GDP of the corresponding country. The weights for UCR-1, the first-generation unicurn, are now fixed and will never change. The value of one first-generation unicurn is expected to decrease over time due to probable inflation of the currencies it refers to.

There are currently no concrete plans to define more generations of unicurn (UCR-2, UCR-3, etc), but one option would be to define one new generation each year.
